BLE & USB MIDI BRIDGE

Your piano,
connected to everything.

Shelementyev’s BLE‑MIDI Bridge is a dependable Bluetooth and USB MIDI bridge for your digital piano — route it into Synthesia, DAWs, and MIDI files, with a live visualizer and recording. And uniquely, it lets a Bluetooth piano play in Roblox.

FREE  ·  FOR WINDOWS  ·  NO ACCOUNT NEEDED

Works with any Bluetooth or USB MIDI keyboard — any size, weighted or not, pedals or not.

Everything in one window

Connect, choose how you play, and watch every note land — in real time.

THE PART NOTHING ELSE DOES

Bluetooth pianos, now playing in Roblox.

Every other way of playing a Roblox piano with real keys needs a USB cable. Because this bridges over Bluetooth as well, your wireless piano can finally play Piano Rooms, Visual Pianos, and the letter-key games. Two output modes cover them all — MidiConnect for full velocity and pedal, QWERTY output for everything else.

WHAT IT DOES

One small app, the whole signal chain.

Piano in — out to your software, your MIDI files, or Roblox.

BLUETOOTH

Bluetooth, finally

Connect a BLE digital piano straight to your PC — no cables, no virtual drivers, no workarounds. This is the part nothing else does.

USB

Wired when it counts

Prefer a cable? USB mode is rock-solid and handles the fastest pieces flawlessly, with the same one-click setup.

ROBLOX

Two ways into Roblox

MidiConnect drives Piano Rooms and Visual Pianos with full velocity and pedal. QWERTY output plays the many letter-key piano games — sharps on Shift, sustain on Space.

MIDI FILES

Play any .mid

Load a MIDI file and send it to your piano or straight into a game. Tempo, loop, and transpose are right there.

SYNTHESIA

Synthesia & DAWs

Pass your piano through to other music software — a built-in MIDI port on Mac/Linux, or loopMIDI on Windows.

LIVE

See and record

A live 88-key visualizer lights up as you play, and one click records your performance to a .mid file.

ROBLOX QUICK-START

Into Roblox in three steps.

1

Connect your piano

Scan for it over Bluetooth, or plug in by USB, and hit Connect.

2

Pick a Roblox type

MidiConnect for Piano Rooms or Visual Pianos, or QWERTY output for letter-key games. Press Test to be sure.

3

Play

Keep the game focused and play. F8 clears a stuck key any time, without leaving Roblox.
